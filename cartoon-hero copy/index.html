Bojack Horseman

A fantastic page about Bojack Horseman

A cartoon character voiced fantastically by will arnett, who is a hollywood actor which had his '15 minutes of fame' in a 90's TV show called "Horsin' around". His peers constantly remind him that he is a has-been, but he begs to differ.

Bojack's characteristics

      -He is depressed as fuck.
      -He's considered immature by his peers, and can't hold a relationship.
      -He is remarkably prone to drug addiction.
      -He tries to fill the gaping hole in his life with meaningless sex, drugs and alcohol.
      -Despite his issues, he's a kind person and he cares deeply for those near him.
      -Even though he cares of his peers, he acts selfish all the time.
      -He's pretty relateable.

The TV show has 5 seasons on Netflix. Another is confirmed for 2019.

Bojack's friends

      Todd Chavez, his friend who he met at a party and who has crashed on his couch for years. Voiced by Aaron Paul.
      Sarah Lynn, who died while on a several month long bender with Bojack. Voiced by Kristen Schaal.
      Princess Carolyn, his agent but also semi-lover, voiced by Amy Sedaris.
      Diane Nguyen, his best friend who supports him throughout his daily struggles. Voiced byAlison Brie.
      Mr. Peanut butter, who is friends with Bojack. Although, Bojack tries to avoid him as much as possible. Voiced by Paul F. Tompkins
